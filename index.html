Welcome to OASIS

A small, calm space just for you.

If life has felt loud lately — this is your pause. OASIS was created for the women who hold everything together: the ones who are tired of being "fine", the ones who haven't had a moment to themselves in months, the ones quietly running on empty.

We host intimate weekend retreats in the English countryside. Two nights of slow mornings, heart-opening conversations, nature walks and wellness — held in beautiful estates with spas, pools and acres of landscaped gardens.

The Reset Retreat · Sept 2026

The Reset Retreat

For women in need of a break.

A quiet weekend in the Berkshire countryside for women who are stressed, overwhelmed, too busy or simply exhausted. The venue sits within 250 acres of landscaped gardens, with a swimming pool, sauna, gym and nine lakes. Slow mornings, fun group discussions, long walks and early nights — all completely optional.

Where De Vere Wokefield Estate, Reading
Duration 2 nights · Check-in 4pm
For Women needing "me time"
  • En-suite single or double rooms · free WiFi
  • Breakfast, lunch & dinner · unlimited tea & coffee
  • Free use of sauna, pool, gym & tennis courts
  • Walks across the beautiful grounds & lakes
  • Journaling, creative reflection & fun activities
  • Plenty of free time — everything is optional
